# Maintenance schedule

This schedule outlines routine service intervals for the LNF‑equipped 2010 Cobalt SS with the F35 manual transmission.  Always verify torque specifications and intervals against the factory service manual and adjust based on driving conditions (e.g., track use vs. daily driving).  All torque values are provided in N·m and lb‑ft.

| Interval (km/mi) | Service task                                        | Tools & Notes |
|------------------|------------------------------------------------------|--------------|
| 5 000 km / 3 000 mi | Engine oil & filter change (Dexos 5W‑30)            | 15 mm socket for drain plug; torque drain plug to 25 N·m (18 lb‑ft). Inspect for metal shavings. |
| 10 000 km / 6 000 mi | Check serpentine belt condition & tensioner         | Visual inspection; replace if cracks/fraying. |
| 20 000 km / 12 000 mi | Replace engine air filter (aftermarket high‑flow)  | Torx T20 screwdriver; inspect MAF sensor. |
| 30 000 km / 18 000 mi | Transmission fluid change (GM synchromesh)         | 17 mm drain/fill; torque drain to 25 N·m. Use 2.5 L fluid. |
| 50 000 km / 30 000 mi | Coolant flush & thermostat check                   | Vacuum fill to avoid air pockets; torque housing bolts to 10 N·m. |
| 80 000 km / 50 000 mi | Spark plug replacement (NGK ILTR6F9G)              | 16 mm spark plug socket; gap 0.028 in; torque to 20 N·m (15 lb‑ft). |
| 100 000 km / 60 000 mi | Timing chain inspection                            | Remove front cover; check tensioner and guides; replace if wear. |
| As needed         | HP Tuners file update                               | Back up stock calibration; increment version and record in `tuning_notes.md`. |

**Note:** For track use or high‑boost calibrations, shorten oil change and spark plug intervals.  Always use high‑quality fuel (at least 94 RON) to prevent detonation and monitor knock retard via HP Tuners.
